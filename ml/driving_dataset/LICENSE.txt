Data Attribution Guidelines
===========================
From our point of view, a reference to the initial publication 
(see below) and a link back to our website are sufficient to meet the 
attribution criterion.

If you use our data set, we would also like to encourage you to send
us a notification about your usage (maybe even a link to your project
that we can use on our website). We are also interested in receiving
a copy of the derived works, results, and corresponding publications.
We are happy to answer any questions or address any remarks related 
to out data set.

The recommended form of attribution for the hciLab Driving Data Set is:

    "This [title of report or article or dataset or service] contains data 
    from the hciLab Driving Data Set ([Reference], http://www.hcilab.org/automotive)
    made available under Open Database License (ODbL 1.0) and Database Contents 
    License (DbCL 1.0)." 

The corresponding reference to be cited as mentioned above in the contribution 
text is:

Heinrich. 2013. A data set of real world driving to assess driver workload. In
Proceedings of the 5th International Conference on Automotive User Interfaces
and Interactive Vehicular Applications (AutomotiveUI '13). ACM, New York, NY,
USA, 150-157. DOI=10.1145/2516540.2516561
http://doi.acm.org/10.1145/2516540.2516561
